ISSUES

ISSUES

STRONG POLICE, SAFE NEIGHBOROODS

As Chairman of the Milton First Responders Foundation, Brian already has a strong relationships with our police and emergency services in Milton. He knows for our city to stay safe, it's not enough just to keep crime down - we must maintain a strong working relationships with neighboring jurisdictions to keep our city insulated from crime. Brian will champion coordination between North Fulton's police departments to stop criminals from working in our area.

STANDING FOR MILTON'S CHARACTER

Brian knows what makes our Milton community so special. Our city is an enclave, away from the overdevelopment and urbanization of metro Atlanta. Brian will honor the citizen-drafted land use plan and will never support extending sewer in our city, period.

RESPECTING OUR EQUESTRIAN HERITAGE

From personal experience, Brian understands the importance of Milton's horse community. Working with horses played an important role in the education of his eldest daughter; Brian has seen first-hand the benefits of our equestrian community. He understands equestrian farms are part of what gives Milton its unique charm and he will always stand to protect this priceless heritage.

INFRASTRUCTURE THAT WORKS FOR MILTON

Brian understands the delicate balance between improving our infrastructure to keep traffic congestion down and ensuring that noise and impact on our neighborhoods are kept to a minimum. He'll work with city officials and GDOT to ensure projects are completed in a timely, responsible fashion.
